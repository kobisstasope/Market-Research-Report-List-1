<p><strong>The Marine Bearing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rolling Bearing</li><li>Sliding Bearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The marine bearing market comprises two primary types: rolling bearings and sliding bearings. Rolling bearings utilize balls or rollers to minimize friction, offering high efficiency and load capacity, making them suitable for dynamic applications such as ship propulsion systems. In contrast, sliding bearings operate on a smooth surface, relying on lubrication to reduce friction. They are often used in applications with lower speeds and higher loads, such as in marine engines and thrusters. Both types are essential for ensuring reliable marine operations.</p></p>
