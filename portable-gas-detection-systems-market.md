<p><p>Portable gas detection systems encompass various technologies tailored for specific applications. Semiconductor sensors are compact and cost-effective, ideal for detecting specific gases. Electrochemical sensors offer high sensitivity for toxic gases, making them suitable for safety monitoring. Catalytic combustion systems excel in detecting combustible gases through oxidation, while Photoionization Detectors (PID) provide rapid detection of volatile organic compounds. Lastly, infrared sensors are effective in identifying greenhouse gases and hydrocarbons, leveraging light absorption principles for precise measurements. Each type serves distinct market needs in safety and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
